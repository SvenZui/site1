ABOUT

US

Leomill company is a cohesive team consisting
of developers, researchers, and builders with
extensive experience.

 Our team includes highly skilled professionals
who are involved in the assembly and testing of
system samples. This allows us to collaborate
closely with architects and construction
companies during the design phase of buildings
and structures. Our primary specialization is
aluminum profile systems for construction and
renovation projects. We design our systems
based on the requirements and challenges we
encounter daily. Our main focus is on developing a
wide range of profiles or profile systems to meet
the demands and specific requirements of various
industries.
